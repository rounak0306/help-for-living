Why We Exist

     

In Gaza and Palestine, every sunrise is a battle for survival. Families wake up not knowing if they will find food for the day, or if their children will make it through the night without medicine. Bombings have left many homeless, with nothing but broken walls where their dreams once stood. Mothers carry their babies with no milk to give, fathers search endlessly for shelter, and children walk barefoot over rubble with empty stomachs and tearful eyes.

     

Help for Living is not a large organization, it is a small initiative fueled by human compassion. We are not backed by governments or corporations. We are carried only by the kindness of individuals like you, who believe that no child should sleep hungry and no family should be left abandoned in despair.

     

Every donation goes directly to food, medical aid, temporary shelters, and education for children robbed of their classrooms by war. Your contribution is not charity—it is survival, it is dignity, it is hope. Together, we can ease their suffering and remind them they are not forgotten.
